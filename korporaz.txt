Taquart presents
"Korporazka"

32-byte intro for Atari XL/XE
with at least 16 KB RAM
PAL/NTSC compatible

First presented in March 2021
at the Lovebyte Party
http://www.lovebyte.party

Programmed by
Piotr '0xF' Fusik <fox@scene.pl>

KORPORAZ.XEX = 32-byte version
  compatible with every stock XL/XE
KORPORAC.XEX = version also
  compatible with 400/800
  and non-standard OSes and DOSes

Ingredients:

           KORPORAZ.XEX  KORPORAC.XEX
DOS header       6            12
Graphics mode    5            33
Sierpinski      12            25
Transition       1             0
Rainbow          8             9
-------------------------------------
Total:       32 bytes      79 bytes
