Taquart presents
"Dither City"

38-byte intro for Atari XL/XE
with at least 16 KB RAM
PAL/NTSC compatible

First presented in March 2021
at the Lovebyte Party
http://www.lovebyte.party

Programmed by
Piotr '0xF' Fusik <fox@scene.pl>

DITHERCI.XEX = 38-byte version
  compatible with every stock XL/XE

DITHERCC.XEX = version also
  compatible with 400/800
  and non-standard OSes and DOSes

Ingredients:

           DITHERCI.XEX  DITHERCC.XEX
DOS header       6            12
Graphics mode    8            34
Dithering       24            41
-------------------------------------
Total:       38 bytes      87 bytes
